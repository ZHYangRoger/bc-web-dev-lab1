A Summa Philosophy Project on Sexual Desire


What is Summa Theologiae?

From Wikipedia: "The Summa Theologiae, as the
well-known work of Thomas Aquinas, is a compendium
of all of the main theological teachings of the
Catholic Church, intended to be an instructional
guide for Theology students.

Structure for a Summa project: A general question,
an article (topic), two or three objections to the
author's own statement, "on the contrary" which cites
words or works of an expert in this area to support
the author's own opinion, "I answer that" part which
stats the author's own opinion, and the author's reply
to all the previously stated objections.

About This Project

There are two articles: one is "Is sexual desire moral?"
and the other is "Is sexual activity moral?". I will talk about
two objections for each article and state my own idea.
